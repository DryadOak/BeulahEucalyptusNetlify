Welcome to Beulah Eucalyptus

Supplying high quality eucalyptus foliage for three generations, we run a small biodiverse plantation located on the edge of Dartmoor.

Eucalyptus Foliage

We offer a variety of natural foliage as well as a limited selection of flowers. The original eucalyptus plantation has been adapted to grow a wide range of species in a biodiverse, chemical free environment. Whether you're a wholesale customer, florist or individual, we tailor our services to you, with no minimum order.

We usually sell foliage by the stem, in mixed seasonal bunches or as large branches for events and architectural decoration.

This years approximate price for delicate bouquet foliage is:

  • 50p per 1ft stem
  • £1 per 2ft stem
  • £2 per 3ft stem
Large branches, unusual foliages and arrangements priced on order. Please ask for seasonal special offers.

Arrangements, Wreaths & Crafts

We carefully craft bespoke floral arrangements and wreaths. These are unique pieces that require a lot of hours and love, so a reasonable lead time is required. In addition, many of our plants are great for arts and craft, from confetti to perfume. If this interests you, find out more about our arrangements, wreaths and crafts here.

Eucalyptus Infused Oils

Herb and tree infused oils grown and made in the re-wilds of a Beulah plantation in Dartmoor. For perfume, hair and beards, tool care and ritual.Click here for more on the various mixes and concoctions we create.

Other Offers

We can occasionally offer timber, firewood, willow wands and saplings. We also have our Beulah craft collective; we currently have skilled artists working here covering blacksmithing, pottery, carpentry, illustration, sculpture, L.A.R.P. props and upcycled instruments.
